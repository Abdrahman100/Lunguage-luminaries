about Our Club:

The Language Pioneers Club specializes in many activities, including:

  3. Organizing competitions
  1. Commemoration of national days and holidays
  2. A convoy for state high schools to introduce the English language major
  3. University sports
  1. Opening translation branches in all institutes
  2. Language training courses
  3. University radio in English
  1. Podcast
  2. YouTube channel and club website
  3. Encouraging emerging entrepreneurs by assisting them in their projects in the linguistic aspect
